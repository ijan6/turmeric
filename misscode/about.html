    • Bean There, Done That is more than just a coffee shop. It’s a place where you can enjoy the best coffee in town, meet new friends, and have fun. We started our coffee shop in 2023, after traveling around the world and discovering the amazing diversity and richness of coffee culture. We wanted to share our love and knowledge of coffee with our community, and create a cozy and friendly space where everyone feels welcome and happy. Our mission is to provide you with the finest and freshest coffee, sourced from ethical and sustainable farms around the world. We roast our own coffee in small batches and brew it with care and precision. We also offer a variety of delicious and healthy food options, such as sandwiches, salads, soups, and desserts. Whether you need a quick pick-me-up, a relaxing break, or a productive work session, Bean There, Done That is the place for you. We have comfortable seating, free Wi-Fi, and a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Come and join us at Bean There, Done That, where every cup is made with love.
